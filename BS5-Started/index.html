max-width

.container irá redimensionar o max-width de acordo com os break-points específicos

  • Extra-small = 100%
  • Small = sm = 540px
  • Medium = md = 720px
  • Large = lg = 960px
  • Extra Large = xl = 1200px
  • XXL = xxl = 1320px
